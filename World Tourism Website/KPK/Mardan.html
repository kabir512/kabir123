MARDAN

  • Discription:-

    • Mardan is a historic city located in the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province of Pakistan. Known for its rich cultural heritage and historical significance, Mardan holds a prominent place in the region. The city is surrounded by lush green fields and picturesque landscapes, adding to its natural beauty. Mardan is home to several educational institutions, including the renowned Abdul Wali Khan University, contributing to its reputation as an educational hub. The city's friendly residents, bustling markets, and traditional cuisine make Mardan a vibrant and welcoming destination for visitors from all over.
