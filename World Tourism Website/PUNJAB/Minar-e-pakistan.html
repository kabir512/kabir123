MINAR-E-PAKISTAN

  • Discription:-

    • Minar-e-Pakistan is a historic monument located in Lahore, Pakistan. It holds immense significance as it symbolizes the nation's struggle for independence. Standing tall at a height of 92 meters, it was built to commemorate the Lahore Resolution of 1940. The structure is a blend of Mughal and modern architectural styles, adorned with intricate calligraphy and beautiful marble work. Minar-e-Pakistan serves as a popular tourist attraction, attracting visitors from around the world who come to admire its grandeur and pay homage to the historic event it represents.
