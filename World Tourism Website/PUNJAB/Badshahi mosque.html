BADSHAHI MOSQUE

  • Discription:-

    • The Badshahi Mosque, located in Lahore, Pakistan, is a magnificent architectural marvel that holds significant historical and cultural importance. Constructed during the Mughal era in 1673, it stands as one of the largest mosques in the world. The mosque's grandeur is evident in its stunning red sandstone and marble structure, adorned with intricate carvings and delicate calligraphy. Its spacious courtyard and prayer hall can accommodate thousands of worshippers at a time. With its majestic domes and towering minarets, the Badshahi Mosque is a testament to the rich architectural heritage of the Mughal Empire.
