    QUETTA

  • Discription:-

    • Quetta, the capital city of Balochistan province in Pakistan, is a city of enchanting beauty and rich cultural heritage. Nestled amidst majestic mountains, Quetta is blessed with breathtaking landscapes and a pleasant climate. The city is known for its famous fruit orchards, particularly apples, and vibrant bazaars filled with handicrafts and traditional Balochi attire. Quetta is also a gateway to the stunning Ziarat Valley, home to the iconic Quaid-e-Azam Residency. The people of Quetta are warm and hospitable, adding to the city's charm. With its unique blend of natural wonders and cultural treasures, Quetta truly captures the hearts of its visitors
