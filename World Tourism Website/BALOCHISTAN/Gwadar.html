GWADAR

  • Discription:-

    • Gwadar, located on the southwestern coast of Pakistan, is a rapidly emerging port city with immense economic potential. Situated near the Strait of Hormuz, it serves as a key access point to the Arabian Sea and is strategically positioned along major international trade routes. Gwadar Port, operated by China Overseas Ports Holding Company, has undergone significant development, transforming the region into a bustling hub of maritime activity and trade. With its deep-water port and state-of-the-art infrastructure, Gwadar is attracting investments from around the world, leading to the establishment of industries, logistics centers, and a free trade zone. The city's promising future as an important trade and energy corridor is set to bring about socio-economic growth and development for both Pakistan and the broader region.
